Frequently asked questions

What is "Chroma Fusion"?

Chroma Fusion is a stock photos and media website from where you can download photos and use them royalty-free. For example, if you're using one of our photos on YT, you won't receive any copyright claims on your YouTube video/short.

Why use Chroma Fusion?

You should give a try to use Chroma Fusion. We have plenty of photos divided accordingly to their respective Themes section. We are giving our best to upload dozens of photos ever 12 hours or so.

How does support work?

We're aware of the importance of well qualified support, that is why we decided that support will only be provided by the authors that actually worked on this project.

Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

I want to apply for CF Partnership. How do I do that?

Make sure you have good amount of followers, appropriate likes and comments on each post. Send us your social media page so that we know who you are. Then, the devs will take the decision further and promote you to be a CF Partner. (All these process are done manually and is usually finished within 2 working days)

Find out more information by reading the license.

What does "free membership" include?

The free membership includes basic features needed to use Chroma Fusion. To get better features, one has to upgrade their account. But honestly, we suppose that the Free plan should be suficient enough for normal usage. (Please note: User must create their account in order to join or apply for specific memberships)

Help. My artwork is uploaded by someone else and they got more recognition!!

Don't worry. Our moderation team monitors every single media uploaded chronologically. So that others won't gain unfair advantages in Chroma Fusion.

How do I work at Chroma Fusion?

Apologies, we aren't currently seeking for more devs or people to work in our team. But hey, you can still donate/purchase memberships to support us though :)

How can my FAQ be in the website?

Join our discord server and ask your question in the #faqs channel. We will manually check and if approved your FAQ will be displayed here.
